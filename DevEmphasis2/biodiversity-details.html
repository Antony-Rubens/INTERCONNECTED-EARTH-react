Why Biodiversity Matters

Biodiversity ensures ecosystem productivity and resilience. It is essential for the survival of all species, including humans.

Benefits of Biodiversity:

  • Supports food security and health.
  • Provides ecosystem services that contribute to human well-being.
  • Enhances ecosystem resilience to environmental changes.
