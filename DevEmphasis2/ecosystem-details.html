Understanding Ecosystems

Ecosystems are vital for our planet. They provide essential services such as clean air and water, pollination, and climate regulation. Healthy ecosystems contribute to the well-being of humans and all life on Earth.

Key Ecosystem Services:

  • Provisioning Services: food, fresh water, fuel, and fiber.
  • Regulating Services: climate regulation, flood control, and disease regulation.
  • Supporting Services: nutrient cycling and soil formation.
  • Cultural Services: recreation, aesthetic enjoyment, and spiritual value.

Learn more about conservation efforts and the importance of protecting these vital ecosystems. For more resources, visit Nature Conservancy.
