The Impact of Climate Change

Climate change is causing severe impacts on our planet, from rising sea levels to extreme weather events. Understanding these changes is crucial.

Key Impacts of Climate Change:

  • Increased temperatures globally.
  • More frequent and severe weather events (hurricanes, floods).
  • Melting polar ice caps and rising sea levels.
  • Disruption of ecosystems and wildlife habitats.

To learn about what can be done, visit United Nations Climate Change.
